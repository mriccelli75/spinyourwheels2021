Get Involved & Donate Now

One of our core beneficiaries of Spin Your Wheels fundraising is Riding for the Handicapped of Western Pennsylvania, Inc. If you are not attending a Spin Your Wheels event via zoom or in person classes, you can always make a tax-deductible donation and help Riding for the Handicapped. RHWPA INC. is a therapeutic horseback riding program that has helped children and young adults with disabilities for more than 30 years. It is staffed entirely by volunteers and funded exclusively by donations, fundraising, and grants. We have over 100 riders (ranging in age from three years to young adult; maximum age 18) participating in 45-minute therapy sessions.
